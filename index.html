About Me

Recording & Performing Artist

Takeem is a Kenyan Rapper, Recording & Performing Artist, born and raised in the slums of Nyalenda-Kisumu. He passionately fell in love with music at a young age. Takeem has cited several Hip Hop Artist as influencing his rapping style including Octopizzo, Lil Wayne, Drake and Jay Z. Takeem's style of Rap is commonly a combination of Swahili Sheng, English and His Vernacular.
